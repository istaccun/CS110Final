Welcome to the Network!

The First Gen Network (FGN) is a Wellesley-specific initiative developed to aid first generation students in the transition to college. First gen students, we hope that you find the resources, stories, and contact information on this website particularly useful! Friends of first gen students, you can check out what we've been up to in the Media tab! Faculty, staff, and students, you can all submit your stories about being first gen to our Stories tab! We hope you enjoy the site, and please feel free to email us with questions or suggestions.
