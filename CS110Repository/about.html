Our History

We were founded in 2013 by Karen Shih and Don Leach, who wanted to create a network of resources for first generation students at Wellesley. Together, with the help of their student coordinators, they built the Network to be the first gen community that Wellesley so needed.

Our Mission

The goals of the First Generation Network are:

  1. To connect First Gen Students to one another and by so doing build a network of peer resources
  2. To connect First Gen Students to faculty and staff and by so doing build a network of mentors and mentees
  3. To extend the network into First Gen Wellesley Alumni and to seek their assistance in addressing the concerns of employment and further education after Wellesley
  4. To connect First Gen Student experiences to the diversity/ inclusion goals of the college and division: issues of class , ethnicity, gender, and citizenship not only intersect with but also provide integrative focus for the curricular and co-curricular aspects of a Wellesley College education
